About Us

At Mr. Coffee Bean, we’re passionate about brewing the best coffee in town.

What started as a small neighborhood café in 2010 has blossomed into a cozy community hub where coffee lovers, creatives, students, and remote workers gather. We were founded with a simple goal: to bring people together over exceptional coffee and meaningful conversations.

Our beans are sourced directly from small-scale, sustainable farms around the globe—from the lush hills of Ethiopia to the rich valleys of Colombia. Every batch is carefully roasted in-house to ensure peak flavor, aroma, and freshness. Whether you prefer a classic espresso or something bold and experimental, our baristas craft each cup with care and precision.

But Mr. Coffee Bean is more than just great coffee. We believe in nurturing creativity and connection. That’s why we host weekly events like live music nights, poetry slams, and barista workshops. Our café walls proudly showcase art from local artists, and our space is designed to feel like a second home—with warm lighting, comfy seating, and the soothing scent of freshly ground beans.

We also care deeply about our planet. All our takeaway cups and packaging are 100% compostable, and we offer discounts to customers who bring reusable mugs. We're proud to be part of a movement that values sustainability, quality, and community.

So whether you're diving into your next novel, working on a passion project, or just grabbing your daily caffeine fix, we invite you to relax and stay awhile. Welcome to Mr. Coffee Bean—where every cup tells a story.
